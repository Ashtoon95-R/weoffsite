OPTI Digital organized their first in-person retreat in the Pyrenees with full support from We Offsite. The natural setting was key to disconnecting from remote day-to-day work and reconnecting as a team.

We managed accommodation at a hotel with meeting rooms, outdoor team-building activities including snowshoeing, and private dinners. Transport logistics from Barcelona and other cities were handled seamlessly.

For OPTI Digital, the retreat marked a before and after in team cohesion. Meeting in person, after months of remote collaboration, consolidated relationships and aligned the company vision.
