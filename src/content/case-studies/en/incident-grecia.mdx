Incident.io chose Athens as the destination for their annual engineering retreat. We Offsite coordinated accommodation, workspaces, cultural activities, and travel logistics for a team distributed across several countries.

Simplicity was the priority: a single point of contact, a clear schedule, and quick responses to any unforeseen issues. The Incident.io team could dedicate their energy to technical sessions and team dynamics.

The retreat structure — morning work sessions, afternoon exploration — demonstrated that well-planned corporate retreats deliver real value beyond social networking.
