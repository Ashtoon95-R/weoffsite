Paperlike needed a corporate event in Barcelona that reflected the quality and attention to detail of their own brand. We Offsite managed venue search, catering, transportation, and technical support for product presentations.

Planning began with a deep briefing on event goals: connecting the remote team, presenting the annual roadmap, and celebrating recent milestones. Every decision — from workspace to social activities — aligned with those objectives.

Team feedback was unanimous: the organization was flawless and enabled a memorable experience without the usual operational burden of corporate events.
