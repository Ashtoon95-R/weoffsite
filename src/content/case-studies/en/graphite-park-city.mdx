Graphite brought 80 people to Park City for a four-day corporate retreat at Stein Eriksen Lodge — one of the largest and most ambitious offsites We Offsite has delivered to date.

From venue negotiation and room blocks to multi-day programming, group meals, and on-site coordination, Roberto and the We Offsite team managed every layer of logistics so Graphite's leadership could focus on their people.

The result was a seamless, high-impact offsite that participants described as one of the most memorable company events they had ever experienced.
