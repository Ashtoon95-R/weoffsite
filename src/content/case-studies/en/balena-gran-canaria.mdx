Balena returned to We Offsite after a previous experience with another agency. For their retreat in Gran Canaria, they sought a partner who understood remote team needs and offered truly comprehensive service.

The program combined strategy sessions, beach activities, and free time to explore the island. We Offsite negotiated group rates, managed bookings, and maintained a direct communication channel throughout the process.

The comparison with their previous experience was clear: greater proactivity, better communication, and a result that exceeded the People & Culture team's expectations.
